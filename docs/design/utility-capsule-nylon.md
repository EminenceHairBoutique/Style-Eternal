# UTILITY CAPSULE — NYLON / TECHNICAL

## Design System

---

## Concept Line
> "Engineered for movement. Built for permanence."

The Utility Capsule is a separate limited release — not tied to the emotional narrative of Drop 02 or Drop 03. This is about the garment itself. Premium utility streetwear where the construction, material, and hardware tell the story. Minimal graphics. Maximum craft.

---

## Direction Statement
The Utility Capsule sits between technical outerwear and luxury streetwear. Every seam has purpose. Every pocket functions. Every panel is considered. The branding is tonal and subtle — "Style Eternal" and "Eternal Utility" marks placed where they serve the garment, not where they compete with it.

This is the collection for people who care about how things are made.

## Garment Concepts

### Nylon Pant 1: "ETERNAL UTILITY" — Hero Pant
- **Silhouette:** Relaxed straight leg with slight taper, articulated knee
- **Material:** Heavyweight nylon ripstop, 190gsm
- **Color:** Jet Black
- **Details:**
  - Dual zip cargo pockets at thigh (YKK zippers)
  - Hidden zip pocket at right rear
  - Articulated knee paneling
  - Adjustable snap tab at ankle
  - Elastic drawcord waistband with internal adjusters
  - Tonal "ETERNAL UTILITY" woven label at right waistband
  - SE monogram embossed on snap hardware
- **Branding:** Tonal — woven label at waist, embossed snaps, interior printed label
- **Fit:** Relaxed with taper below knee
- **Price Point:** $175–195

### Nylon Pant 2: "SE TECH" — Slim Utility Pant
- **Silhouette:** Slim-straight, clean profile
- **Material:** Stretch nylon blend (87% nylon / 13% elastane), DWR coated
- **Color:** Olive
- **Details:**
  - Welted zip pockets at hip
  - Single thigh pocket with hidden zip
  - Gusseted crotch for mobility
  - Reflective piping at inner ankle seam (subtle, visible only in motion)
  - Elastic waistband with flat front presentation
  - Tonal SE woven label at back waistband
- **Branding:** Minimal — back waistband label, interior care label
- **Fit:** Slim-straight
- **Price Point:** $155–175

### Shell Jacket: "ETERNAL UTILITY" — Matching Shell
- **Silhouette:** Cropped coach jacket / shell — hip length
- **Material:** Heavyweight nylon matching Pant 1 ripstop
- **Color:** Jet Black (matching Pant 1)
- **Details:**
  - Full front snap placket with hidden zip behind
  - Dual welt pockets at waist
  - Chest zip pocket (left)
  - Adjustable snap cuffs
  - Interior mesh lining
  - Tonal "STYLE ETERNAL" print at back yoke (small, 4" wide)
  - SE monogram embossed on all snap hardware
  - Hang loop with woven label
- **Branding:** Tonal back yoke print, embossed hardware, interior printed label
- **Fit:** Relaxed, cropped to hip
- **Price Point:** $225–265

## Capsule Cohesion

### Full Set Direction
The hero set is **Pant 1 + Shell Jacket** in Jet Black. Designed to be worn as a matching set or separated into individual wardrobe pieces.

When worn together:
- Same nylon ripstop material
- Same tonal branding approach
- Same hardware (SE-embossed snaps, YKK zips)
- Same color: Jet Black
- Creates a technical tracksuit silhouette without looking like athletic wear

### Mix-and-Match
Each piece should also work independently:
- Pant 1 pairs with any hoodie from Drop 02 or Drop 03
- Shell Jacket layers over Essentials tees or crewnecks
- Pant 2 (Olive) provides contrast when paired with black tops

## Branding Rules for Utility

1. **No large graphics.** The garment IS the design.
2. **Tonal marks only.** Same-color-darker for woven labels and prints.
3. **Hardware branding.** SE monogram on snaps, zipper pulls where possible.
4. **Interior branding.** Full printed interior label with care instructions, "ETERNAL UTILITY" branding, and garment story.
5. **Hang tag.** Premium heavy card stock, bone/black, with capsule details.

---

## What to Avoid

- ❌ Large graphic prints on nylon pieces
- ❌ Athletic/sportwear branding approach
- ❌ Loud logos or all-over patterns
- ❌ Technical mountaineering aesthetics
- ❌ Reflective tape or high-vis elements (except subtle piping)
- ❌ Velcro closures
- ❌ Multiple colorblocking panels
- ❌ Fast-fashion nylon (use heavyweight, quality fabrication)
