# OUTERWEAR / HOODIE DESIGN DIRECTIONS

## Design Briefs

---

## 1. Eternal Struggle Hero Hoodie

### Brief
The centerpiece of Drop 02. A title-led back graphic featuring the cracked crown symbol — power that doesn't hold. The front is minimal (SE monogram), letting the back do all the talking.

### Specifications
- **Garment:** Pullover hoodie, oversized, 400gsm French terry
- **Colorway:** Washed Black (#1C1C1C)
- **Front:** SE monogram, left chest, 2.5" wide, bone embroidery
- **Back:** Cracked crown graphic (12" wide × 14" tall) with "ETERNAL STRUGGLE" title arching above in condensed gothic. Broken scales beneath crown. Bone + silver chrome accent.
- **Hood Interior:** "SE" embroidery, 1" wide
- **Sleeve:** None
- **Print Method:** Screen print (bone) + foil (silver chrome, selective)
- **Price:** $155

### Composition Notes
- Crown should be abstract/stylized — not a literal royal crown
- Cracks radiate from center of crown
- Scales beneath are tilted and fragmented, not realistic
- Title is the dominant element — crown supports, doesn't compete
- Leave 2–3" of clear space below neckline before back graphic begins

## 2. Eternal Love Hero Hoodie

### Brief
Evolution of the existing Eternal Love Hoodie. All-over print with skeleton figure and flame sleeves. The most graphic-heavy piece in the brand — statement outerwear for the romantic rebel.

### Specifications
- **Garment:** Pullover hoodie, oversized, 400gsm heavyweight cotton fleece
- **Colorway:** Black (#0A0A0A)
- **Front:** "ETERNAL LOVE" title with chrome heart emblem above, chest center
- **Back:** Skeleton figure cradling/embracing form — central composition. Flames rising from bottom edge.
- **Sleeves:** Pink-red flame wraps on both sleeves (continuous from body)
- **Print Method:** All-over screen print (multi-color: bone, red bright, deep red, gold)
- **Price:** $160

### Composition Notes
- Skeleton figure is dignified, not horror — devotional pose
- Flames rise upward — suggesting eternal fire, not destruction
- Flame colors: gradient from deep red (#8B2020) at base to red bright (#C43030) at tips with bone (#E8E4DE) highlights
- Chrome heart emblem above title is small (2") — luxury accent
- This is the "collector's piece" of Drop 03

## 3. Eternal Flame Hoodie

### Brief
A flame-focused piece that bridges between the Eternal Love emotional range and a cleaner, more wearable graphic. The flame contains a heart form — love and fire unified.

### Specifications
- **Garment:** Pullover hoodie, oversized, 400gsm French terry
- **Colorway:** Off Black (#1A1917)
- **Front:** "ETERNAL FLAME" stacked title in condensed gothic, chest center, 6" wide
- **Back:** Large single flame graphic (10" wide × 14" tall) with heart shape integrated into flame core. "ETERNAL FLAME" title integrated into base.
- **Hood Interior:** "SE" embroidery
- **Print Method:** Screen print (red bright + bone + deep red)
- **Price:** $150

### Composition Notes
- Flame is a single unified form — not a scatter of small flames
- Heart shape within flame should be visible but not obvious — organic integration
- Title at flame base should feel like it's anchoring the flame, not floating
- Grain texture overlay on flame for depth

## 4. Forbidden Love Continuation Hoodie

### Brief
Continuation of the existing Forbidden Love design language. Skeleton embrace with broken hearts. This version refines the original while maintaining the brand DNA.

### Specifications
- **Garment:** Pullover hoodie, oversized, 400gsm French terry, garment washed
- **Colorway:** Washed Black (#1C1C1C)
- **Front:** "FORBIDDEN LOVE" title with skeleton embrace graphic beneath. Broken heart elements.
- **Back:** "LOVE IS ETERNAL, JUST LIKE STYLE" in accent type, small at upper back. Subtle flame accents at lower back.
- **Print Method:** Screen print (bone + red bright)
- **Price:** $145

### Composition Notes
- Maintains existing Forbidden Love aesthetic
- Skeleton embrace is two figures — intertwined, not separated
- Broken hearts are cracked, not shattered — restraint matters

## 5. SE Worldwide / Core Identity Outerwear Concept

### Brief
The brand's core identity hoodie — not tied to any single drop. "SE Worldwide" represents the brand's aspiration beyond Newark. Crest-driven, branded, wearable year-round.

### Specifications
- **Garment:** Pullover hoodie, relaxed fit, 380gsm French terry
- **Colorway:** Black (#0A0A0A) — also available in Bone (#E8E4DE)
- **Front:** SE crest graphic, chest center. Combines SE monogram with laurel wreath or geometric frame.
- **Back:** "STYLE ETERNAL EST. 2021" medallion graphic with "WORLDWIDE" beneath in wide-tracked accent type
- **Sleeves:** Gold star accents on left sleeve
- **Print Method:** Screen print (bone on black / black on bone) + gold foil accents
- **Price:** $135

### Composition Notes
- Crest is the brand's official seal — needs to feel premium, not collegiate
- "EST. 2021" grounds the brand in time without being nostalgic
- "WORLDWIDE" positions the brand globally
- Stars are small, scattered, architectural — not decorative
- This piece should feel like a uniform — the hoodie you wear when you represent SE

## 6. Essentials Hoodie / Crewneck — Minimal Concept

### Brief
The purest expression of Style Eternal. No graphics, no narrative — just the monogram and the weight of the garment. The daily uniform.

### Specifications (Hoodie)

- **Colorway:** Black (#0A0A0A), Grey (#4A4A4A), Light Grey (#9B9B9B)
- **Front:** SE monogram embroidery, left chest, 2" wide
- **Back:** None
- **Print Method:** Embroidery only
- **Price:** $120

### Specifications (Crewneck)
- **Garment:** Crewneck sweatshirt, relaxed fit, 380gsm French terry
- **Colorway:** Black (#0A0A0A), Grey (#4A4A4A)
- **Front:** SE monogram embroidery, left chest, 2" wide
- **Back:** None
- **Print Method:** Embroidery only
- **Price:** $110

### Design Notes
- Monogram thread color: tone-on-tone (charcoal on black, dark grey on grey, medium grey on light grey)
- No tags visible at neckline — woven label at interior side seam
- This is the "quality test" piece — if you pick this up, you should feel the weight immediately

## 7. Premium Utility Outerwear — Nylon Shell Concept

### Brief
Bridges the Utility Capsule and the core hoodie lineup. A nylon shell jacket that pairs with the Eternal Utility nylon pants or layers over any drop hoodie.

### Specifications
- **Garment:** Cropped coach jacket / shell, hip length, nylon ripstop
- **Colorway:** Jet Black (#0A0A0A), Charcoal (#3A3A3A)
- **Front:** Full snap placket with hidden zip behind. Dual welt pockets. Chest zip pocket (left).
- **Back:** "STYLE ETERNAL" tonal print at back yoke, 4" wide
- **Sleeves:** Adjustable snap cuffs
- **Interior:** Mesh lining, printed interior label
- **Branding:** Tonal prints, SE-embossed snap hardware, woven label at back neck
- **Price:** $225–265

### Design Notes
- This is garment-design-driven, not graphic-driven
- The material, hardware, and construction tell the story
- Think: what would a premium version of a Nike ACG shell look like if it had streetwear DNA instead of outdoor DNA
- SE monogram on all snaps — small, embossed
- Interior label should have capsule branding: "ETERNAL UTILITY — STYLE ETERNAL — SS26"
