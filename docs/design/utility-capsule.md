# Utility Capsule — Design Direction

> **Theme:** Technical Luxury · Premium Utility · Seam Architecture · Silhouette-First
> **Season:** FW26 (releasing 4–6 weeks after Drop 01 restock)
> **Position:** A separate limited release — no heavy graphics, all construction and restraint
> **Naming candidates:** "Eternal Utility" · "Style Eternal Utility Release" · "Utility Capsule 01" · "Style Eternal Technical Release"

---

## Capsule Overview

The Utility Capsule is Style Eternal's first move into **technical garments and silhouette-driven design**. This is not a graphic drop — it's a construction drop.

The emphasis shifts from screenprinted collages to:
- Seam architecture and panel logic
- Pocket placement and hardware emphasis
- Tonal branding and subtle text hits
- Fabric and silhouette as the design language
- Premium restraint

This capsule should feel like the **utility wing** of the brand — still recognizable as Style Eternal, but built for a buyer who wants the brand's identity expressed through cut and construction rather than print.

---

## Visual Language

### Design Principles

| Principle | Expression |
|---|---|
| **Silhouette-first** | The garment's shape is the design — tapered legs, articulated knees, panel seams |
| **Tonal branding** | "STYLE ETERNAL" and "SE" appear as tone-on-tone prints, woven labels, or embossed details |
| **Hardware emphasis** | YKK zippers, snap closures, D-rings, webbing pulls — functional and visible |
| **Seam architecture** | Contrast stitching, panel construction visible from outside, topstitching as design |
| **Minimal symbols** | The cracked crown or SE monogram appears once per garment, small scale |
| **Material language** | Nylon ripstop, stretch-woven nylon, bonded seams, mesh lining |

### Utility Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Black | #0A0A0A | Primary — default for all utility pieces |
| Dark Olive | #3D3D2E | Secondary — military/technical reference |
| Charcoal | #2A2A2A | Alternate — tonal variation |
| Bone / Off-White | #E8E4DE | Accent — interior lining, label details |
| Gold | #D4AF37 | Brand accent — small hardware, woven labels |

### Graphic Restraint Rules

- **No large-format screenprinted back prints**
- **No multi-element collage compositions**
- **No skull/skeleton/flame graphics on utility pieces**
- Branding is embedded through labels, hardware, and tonal hits
- If a symbol appears, it's **one mark, small scale, single placement**

## Garment Concepts

### NYLON PANT 01: "Eternal Utility" Nylon Pant — Black

**Garment:** Nylon pant · Black · Stretch-woven nylon · Relaxed tapered fit
**Concept:** The anchor of the capsule. Utility-forward with premium construction.

**Construction details:**
- Articulated knee panels with visible seam architecture
- 6-pocket configuration: 2 side-seam slash pockets, 2 cargo pockets (zip closure), 2 rear welt pockets
- Elastic waistband with internal drawcord
- Tapered leg with elastic ankle cuffs
- Contrast topstitching in tonal dark grey

**Branding:**
- Left cargo pocket: small woven "STYLE ETERNAL" label, tonal black on black
- Right rear pocket: embroidered "SE" monogram, 1" tall, tonal
- Waistband interior: printed "ETERNAL UTILITY · EST. 2021" on grosgrain tape

**Hardware:** Matte black YKK zippers on cargo pockets, matte metal aglets on drawcord
**Print technique:** Woven labels + tonal embroidery only — no screenprint
**Mood:** Technical. Premium. The nylon pant that doesn't look like every other brand's.
**Why it fits:** Silhouette-first design with embedded branding. The construction is the graphic.

### TECHNICAL JOGGER 01: "Eternal Utility" Tech Jogger

**Garment:** Technical jogger · Black · Bonded nylon-jersey blend · Tapered fit
**Concept:** Hybrid jogger — nylon outer face, jersey inner for comfort. More elevated than a sweatpant.

**Construction details:**
- Bonded fabric — smooth nylon exterior, soft jersey interior
- 4-pocket configuration: 2 on-seam zip pockets, 2 rear zip pockets
- Elastic waistband with flat front panel (no bunching)
- Tapered leg, elastic ankle cuffs
- Internal drawcord with branded aglets

**Branding:**
- Left hip: heat-pressed "SE" mark, tonal, 1.5" tall
- Right rear: woven "STYLE ETERNAL" label
- Drawcord aglets: laser-etched "SE"

**Hardware:** Matte black YKK zippers on all pockets
**Print technique:** Heat press tonal mark + woven labels — no screenprint
**Mood:** Sleek. Modern. The jogger that works with a jacket.
**Why it fits:** Bridges utility and everyday wear. The fabric is the luxury — no print needed.

### SHELL JACKET 01: "Eternal Utility" Shell

**Garment:** Lined shell jacket · Black · Nylon outer, mesh/taffeta lining · Regular fit
**Concept:** The outerwear companion. Technical but not overly sporty.

**Construction details:**
- 2-way front zip (YKK)
- Stand collar with snap-down storm flap
- 2 front welt pockets with zip closure
- 1 interior zip pocket
- Elasticized cuffs with snap adjust
- Back vent with mesh lining
- Tonal seam taping

**Branding:**
- Left chest: embroidered "SE" monogram, 1.5" tall, tonal
- Back neck interior: woven "STYLE ETERNAL · EST. 2021 · UTILITY" label
- Right cuff: small woven flag label, "SE" in gold on black

**No back graphic.** The jacket's silhouette and construction are the statement.

**Hardware:** Matte black YKK 2-way zip, matte snaps, branded zip pull
**Print technique:** Embroidery + woven labels only
**Mood:** Premium technical outerwear. The jacket version of "I don't need to be loud."
**Why it fits:** Every capsule needs outerwear. This anchors the set without competing with the graphic drops.

---

### MATCHING SET DIRECTION: "Eternal Utility" Full Kit

**Concept:** The shell jacket + nylon pant (black) styled as a complete set.

**Set logic:**
- Both pieces share the same matte black hardware finish
- Same tonal branding language (embroidered "SE" + woven labels)
- Same nylon hand-feel
- When worn together: clean technical silhouette, no visual conflict

**Styling guidance:**
- Over an Essentials tee (black or bone) — the utility shell frames the core
- With a graphic hoodie underneath (Eternal Struggle or Eternal Love) — technical meets emotional

**Why it fits:** Matching sets are a market expectation in premium streetwear. This one is construction-led, not logo-led.
