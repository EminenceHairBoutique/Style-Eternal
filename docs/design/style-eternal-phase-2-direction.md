# STYLE ETERNAL — PHASE 2 CREATIVE DIRECTION

## Brand Visual Language

### Position
Luxury streetwear with emotional weight. Modern abstract iconography meets title-led graphics. Newark is the origin story — not the whole identity. The brand speaks globally through emotional resonance, collectible design, and premium material choices.

### Core Mood
- Dark
- Premium
- Youthful
- Romantic
- Rebellious
- Graphic
- Collectible
- Emotionally weighted
- Fashion-forward

## Title System

### Naming Convention
Every piece and drop leads with a **title-first** approach. Names should feel like chapter titles — emotional, concise, and memorable.

### Naming Lanes

| Lane | Examples |
|------|----------|
| **Eternal + [Noun]** | Eternal Struggle, Eternal Love, Eternal Flame, Eternal Unity, Eternal Freedom |
| **[Emotion] Never [Verb]** | Love Never Dies, Love Never Leaves, Hope Never Fades |
| **Forbidden + [Noun]** | Forbidden Love, Forbidden Light |
| **Born + [Preposition]** | Born Against Power, Born in Fire |
| **Single Word Power** | Survival, Legacy, Freedom |
| **SE [Identifier]** | SE Worldwide, SE Utility, SE Essentials |

### Rules
- Titles are always uppercase on garments
- Titles must work as standalone compositions
- Never use more than 4 words in a title
- Avoid cliché streetwear phrases ("Stay True", "No Cap", "Real Ones")

## Logo / Icon Usage

### Brand Mark Hierarchy

1. **Primary Wordmark** — "STYLE ETERNAL" in Oswald/condensed sans, tracked wide
2. **Secondary Wordmark** — "SE" stacked or inline with "STYLE ETERNAL" beneath
3. **Compact Monogram** — "SE" interlocked letterforms
4. **Favicon Symbol** — Abstract flame/eternal loop mark
5. **Garment Stamp** — Small "SE" monogram for chest placement, tags, hardware

### Usage Rules
- Wordmark: website header, campaign banners, large format
- Monogram: garment tags, chest stamps, social avatars
- Favicon: browser tab, app icon, PWA
- Never distort, outline, or apply gradients to the wordmark
- Minimum clear space: half the height of the "S" on all sides
- On dark backgrounds: bone/cream wordmark
- On light backgrounds: black wordmark

## Website Placeholder Direction

### Coming Soon Products
- Dark atmospheric smoke/haze backgrounds
- Subtle gold or chrome accent lines
- Strong title typography centered
- "COMING SOON" or "DROPPING [DATE]" in accent font
- Never use blurry product photos or generic stock

### Collection Banners
- Full-bleed dark gradients with grain texture overlay
- Collection name in display font, large scale
- Tagline in accent font beneath
- Atmospheric photography or abstract texture
- Avoid literal product shots in banner format

### Campaign Teasers
- Cinematic aspect ratios (16:9 or 2.4:1)
- Single symbol or title element
- Smoke, atmospheric haze, muted light
- Drop name + date only
- Premium "teaser poster" energy

## Visual Do / Do Not Rules

### DO
- ✅ Use title-led compositions with bold readable names
- ✅ Keep central hierarchy clean and balanced
- ✅ Use symbolic collage with abstract elements
- ✅ Maintain slight comic-book/anime hint in line quality
- ✅ Keep emotional tone controlled — heavy but not chaotic
- ✅ Use premium washed-black as primary garment base
- ✅ Apply chrome/silver accents carefully
- ✅ Use pink-red as emotional accent sparingly
- ✅ Combine gothic display + clean condensed support type
- ✅ Design icon-driven, collectible visuals
- ✅ Make graphics work at large (back print) and small (chest stamp) scales
- ✅ Reference material archives for realistic silhouettes/colorways

### DO NOT
- ❌ Mall-brand streetwear aesthetics
- ❌ Cheap tattoo tee energy
- ❌ Overworked protest murals
- ❌ Generic "urban" clichés
- ❌ Too much literal storytelling in graphics
- ❌ Over-cluttered compositions
- ❌ Overuse of Newark references
- ❌ Full anime/manga style (hints only)
- ❌ Chrome Hearts direct copies
- ❌ Fake-gothic or biker-brand logo treatment
- ❌ Giant placeholder blocks or blur cards
- ❌ Generic stock mockups
- ❌ Bright/neon colors
- ❌ All-over text patterns
- ❌ Photorealistic illustrations (keep abstract)

## Typography Hierarchy

### Display (Headlines, Titles)
- **Font:** Oswald 600–700
- **Usage:** Page titles, drop names, garment title graphics
- **Style:** All caps, wide tracking (0.04–0.15em)

### Body (Copy, Descriptions)
- **Font:** Inter 300–500
- **Usage:** Product descriptions, body copy, UI text
- **Style:** Sentence case, regular tracking

### Accent (Labels, Meta)
- **Font:** Space Grotesk 400–600
- **Usage:** Category labels, overlines, technical details, dates
- **Style:** All caps, extra-wide tracking (0.15–0.28em), small sizes

### Signature (Decorative)
- **Font:** Great Vibes
- **Usage:** Limited — editorial flourishes, taglines on campaign
- **Style:** Title case, never for body copy

### Garment Typography
- Title graphics: condensed gothic sans (Oswald-style) or custom hand-drawn
- Support text: clean sans-serif (Space Grotesk-style)
- Never use more than 2 type styles in a single garment graphic
- Script fonts only for "Eternal" or "Love" treatments — never for full titles
