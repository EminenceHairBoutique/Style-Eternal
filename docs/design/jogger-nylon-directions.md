# JOGGER / NYLON PANT DESIGN DIRECTIONS

## Design Briefs

---

## 1. Nylon Utility Pant Hero — "ETERNAL UTILITY"

### Brief
The flagship pant of the Utility Capsule. Heavyweight nylon ripstop with articulated construction, multiple zip pockets, and tonal SE branding. This is the trouser version of the brand promise: built with intention, engineered for permanence.

### Specifications
- **Garment:** Relaxed straight leg with taper, articulated knee
- **Material:** Heavyweight nylon ripstop, 190gsm, matte finish
- **Colorway:** Jet Black (#0A0A0A)
- **Details:**
  - Dual zip cargo pockets at thigh (YKK hardware, self-fabric pulls)
  - Hidden zip pocket at right rear
  - Articulated knee paneling (triple-needle stitched)
  - Adjustable snap tab at ankle cuff
  - Elastic drawcord waistband with internal elastic adjusters
  - Belt loops for optional belt wear
  - Bartack reinforcement at all stress points
- **Branding:**
  - Tonal "ETERNAL UTILITY" woven label at right waistband interior
  - SE monogram embossed on all snap hardware
  - Interior printed label with full care + capsule branding
- **Fit:** Relaxed through thigh, tapered below knee
- **Price:** $185

### Construction Notes
- Knee articulation should be functional — genuine mobility improvement, not decorative stitching
- Pocket zips should be smooth-operating, not scratchy
- Snap tab at ankle allows for both loose and cinched silhouettes
- Waistband should sit at natural waist, not low-rise

### Silhouette Reference
Based on the uploaded nylon jogger references. Key difference from jogger silhouette: this is a pant (straight leg with taper), not a jogger (no elastic cuff). More architectural, less athletic.

## 2. Technical Jogger — "SE TECH"

### Brief
A slim-profile utility jogger in stretch nylon. Cleaner than the hero pant, designed for daily wear with subtle technical details. The elevated jogger for people who have outgrown sweatpants but still want comfort.

### Specifications
- **Garment:** Slim-straight jogger with elastic cuff
- **Material:** Stretch nylon blend (87% nylon / 13% elastane), DWR coated, 170gsm
- **Colorway:** Olive (#5C553B)
- **Details:**
  - Welted zip pockets at hip (invisible when closed)
  - Single thigh pocket with hidden zip (right leg)
  - Gusseted crotch for mobility
  - Reflective piping at inner ankle seam (visible only in motion/flash)
  - Elastic waistband with drawcord and flat front presentation
  - Elastic cuff at ankle
- **Branding:**
  - Tonal SE woven label at back waistband
  - Interior printed label
  - No exterior graphics
- **Fit:** Slim-straight, slight taper to elastic cuff
- **Price:** $165

### Construction Notes
- DWR coating provides light water resistance — not waterproof
- Gusseted crotch is essential for the slim profile — without it, mobility is compromised
- Reflective piping is a safety/luxury detail — 3M-style but subtle
- Flat front presentation means no visible elastic gather when standing straight

### Silhouette Reference
Closer to a dressed-up jogger than a cargo pant. Think: the pant you can wear to dinner and then ride your bike home in. Based on uploadedacksuit references for proportions.

## 3. Fleece Jogger Companion — Drop 02: "STRUGGLE" Jogger

### Brief
The fleece jogger that completes any Drop 02 hoodie outfit. Minimal branding, heavyweight French terry, designed to match the Eternal Struggle hoodies in weight and proportion. Not a statement piece — a supporting player.

### Specifications
- **Garment:** Relaxed tapered jogger with elastic cuff
- **Material:** 360gsm French terry, brushed interior
- **Colorway:** Washed Black (#1C1C1C)
- **Details:**
  - Side seam pockets (no zip)
  - Single back patch pocket (right)
  - Elastic drawcord waistband
  - Elastic ribbed cuffs at ankle
  - Flat-lock seam construction
- **Branding:**
  - Right thigh: SE monogram embroidery (bone thread, 1.5" wide)
  - Left thigh: "STRUGGLE" in condensed type embroidery (bone thread, 3" wide)
  - Interior printed label
- **Fit:** Relaxed through thigh, tapered to cuff
- **Price:** $120

### Design Notes
- This is a companion to the Eternal Struggle hoodies — same fabric weight (360gsm matches)
- Washed Black colorway matches the hero hoodies
- Embroidery should be subtle — not dominant when worn
- "STRUGGLE" text is small enough to be a detail, not a billboard

### Outfit Logic
- Pairs with: Eternal Struggle Hoodie, No Kings Hoodie, Eternal Unity Hoodie
- Also works with: Essentials tees, SE Worldwide hoodie
- Does NOT pair with: Utility Capsule nylon pieces (different material language)

## 5. Matching Set / Tracksuit Direction

### Brief
The full matching set combines the Shell Jacket + Nylon Pant 1 from the Utility Capsule. Not designed as a traditional tracksuit — this is a utility suit. Technical streetwear that functions as a complete outfit.

### Specifications
- **Top:** Eternal Utility Shell Jacket (see outerwear-hoodie-directions.md)
- **Bottom:** Eternal Utility Nylon Pant (Pant 1, above)
- **Colorway:** Both in Jet Black (#0A0A0A)
- **Material Match:** Same heavyweight nylon ripstop for both pieces
- **Hardware Match:** SE-embossed snap hardware throughout

### Set-Specific Details
- When sold as bundle: special pricing ($375–425 vs $410–460 individual)
- Ships in premium branded packaging
- Includes exclusive interior-printed "ETERNAL UTILITY — 001 OF 150" label (set-numbered)
- Hang tag specific to the set: "THE UTILITY SET — STYLE ETERNAL — SS26"

### Silhouette When Worn Together
- Cropped jacket ends at hip
- Pant sits at natural waist
- 1–2" of layered garment visible between jacket and pant if worn with tee underneath
- Overall silhouette: clean, architectural, not bulky
- Based on the uploaded tracksuit references — but elevated from athletic to technical streetwear
